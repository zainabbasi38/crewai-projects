Guidelines for Personalized Lead Outreach:

1. Communication Style
   - Keep tone professional yet conversational
   - Be concise and value-focused
   - Demonstrate knowledge of their business

2. Message Structure
   - Open with a relevant observation about their business
   - Connect their needs to our solutions
   - Include specific value propositions
   - End with a clear call to action

3. Personalization Points
   - Reference recent company achievements
   - Mention specific pain points identified
   - Align message with company culture
   - Address decision maker by name and title

4. Follow-up Strategy
   - Plan timing of follow-up messages
   - Vary message content and approach
   - Include relevant new information
   - Maintain professional persistence
