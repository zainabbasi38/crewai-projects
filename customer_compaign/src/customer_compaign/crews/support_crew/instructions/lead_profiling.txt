Research and analyze potential leads with the following guidelines:

1. Company Background
   - Research company history and core business
   - Identify industry position and market share
   - Review recent news and developments

2. Decision Makers
   - Identify key executives and decision makers
   - Research their professional background
   - Note their recent public statements or initiatives

3. Business Needs
   - Analyze current challenges and pain points
   - Identify growth opportunities
   - Map how our solutions align with their needs

4. Recent Developments
   - Track recent company announcements
   - Monitor business expansions or pivots
   - Note any relevant partnerships or acquisitions
